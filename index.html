How to Use This Tierlist

When a Hard Drive offers you a choice, pick the highest-tier recipe available. If both options are from the same tier, use these tiebreakers:

  • Early game (Tiers 0–3): Prioritize Solid Steel Ingot, Cast Screw or Stitched Iron Plate, Coated Iron Plate, Steel Rod, Iron Wire
  • Mid game (Tiers 4–6): Copper Alloy Ingot, Heavy Encased Frame, Caterium Circuit Board, Fused Wire, Steel Screw
  • Late game (Tiers 7–8): Sloppy Alumina, Pure Aluminium Ingot, Crystal Computer, Diluted Fuel, Recycled Plastic/Rubber
  • End game (Nuclear): Uranium Fuel Unit, Infused Uranium Cell, Oil-Based Diamonds

There are ~115 hard drives and ~106 alternate recipes on the map — you can unlock nearly everything eventually. Grab the S-tier recipes first, then fill in the rest.
